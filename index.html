Biography

Dali Yan earned his B.S. degree in Electrical and Information Engineering from Dezhou University, and later completed his M.S. in Materials Science and Engineering at Wuhan Textile University under the guidance of Prof. Bo Deng. During this period, he was also mentored by Academician Weilin Xu and received joint training at the State Key Laboratory.

He is also a big fan of machine learning, the control and sensing of smart wearable devices, and flexible electronic materials. He is particularly passionate about advancing the intelligence of these technologies by integrating foundational model design with deep learning algorithms.
